# Local SEO Checklist For KIT

Use this checklist for local SEO planning and audits. It separates code/site work from owner/manual work.

Do not use this checklist to justify fake reviews, fake addresses, fake ratings, fake locations, or mass doorway district pages.

## Code And Site Tasks

- Keep the homepage and core landing pages clear about service area: Saint Petersburg and Leningrad Region.
- Keep NAP details consistent where visible: company name, phone, email, service area, working hours.
- Ensure important pages have unique title, description, H1, canonical, and indexability.
- Keep sitemap limited to real public pages.
- Add internal links only to existing useful pages.
- Use `BreadcrumbList` where breadcrumbs are visible.
- Use `FAQPage` only when the exact FAQ is visible on the page.
- Do not add fake `Review`, `AggregateRating`, `Product`, `Offer`, or unsupported LocalBusiness claims.
- Keep `/kuhni-na-zakaz-spb`, `/stoimost-kuhni-na-zakaz`, `/uglovye-kuhni`, `/pryamye-kuhni`, and `/sovremennye-kuhni` distinct.
- Do not create mass district/city pages until unique content, review policy, indexability rules, and owner approval exist.

## Owner / Manual Tasks

- Verify Google Search Console for `https://kuhni-kit.ru`.
- Verify Yandex Webmaster for `https://kuhni-kit.ru`.
- Create or update Yandex Business profile with accurate name, phone, website, service area, working hours, photos, and categories.
- Create or update 2GIS profile with the same NAP data.
- Keep VK profile details consistent with the website.
- Check that business categories match custom kitchens / furniture / kitchen furniture services.
- Add real photos only: kitchens, materials, showroom/workshop/process only when accurate.
- Ask real customers for honest reviews after completed work; never buy, invent, or manipulate reviews.
- Track source quality from Yandex Business, 2GIS, VK, search, referrals, and direct contacts.

## Content Assets Needed

- Real project photos or clearly labeled examples/visualizations.
- Accurate service region wording.
- Real warranty/contract wording approved by the owner.
- Real process steps and timing ranges.
- Material/fittings categories actually offered.
- Pricing policy: online estimates are preliminary, final price depends on measurement, design, materials, and selected options.
- Real customer review policy and approved way to quote reviews.

## Citation And Profile Consistency

Keep these consistent across profiles and website:
- business name: KIT / КИТ;
- website: `https://kuhni-kit.ru`;
- phone;
- email if shown;
- service area;
- working hours;
- business category;
- links to Telegram/VK only if they are active and owner-approved.

Avoid:
- old domain variants;
- duplicate profiles with inconsistent phones;
- fake addresses;
- hidden or temporary contact channels;
- unapproved WhatsApp CTA changes.

## Review Policy

- Use only real reviews from real customers.
- Do not fabricate names, ratings, dates, review text, or screenshots.
- Do not add aggregate rating schema unless review data is real, visible, and policy-compliant.
- Do not pressure customers into dishonest reviews.
- If a review is copied to the site, keep source and permission expectations clear.

## Measurement And Monitoring

- Track index coverage in Google Search Console and Yandex Webmaster.
- Track impressions/clicks for core pages and queries.
- Track leads by source in the lead tracker/CRM process.
- Review local profile calls/messages monthly.
- Watch for search queries that indicate missing useful pages.
- Treat ranking drops, wrong snippets, or profile inconsistencies as audit triggers.

## Explicitly Forbidden

- Fake reviews, fake ratings, fake projects, fake counters, fake addresses, or fake locations.
- Doorway pages for every district/city with swapped keywords.
- Unsupported guarantees, production claims, deadlines, or exact final online prices.
- Partial production Directus content filling as a shortcut for local SEO.
- GA4/GTM, new trackers, or production deploy changes unless separately approved.
